FIRST LESSON IS ARITHMETIC.

DEFFINATION:
THE WORD ARITHMATIC REFERS TO FOUR BASICS OPERATIONS ADDITION,SUBTRACTION,MUTIPLICATION ADN DIVISION.

ADDITION:
ADDING SIMILER THINGS LIKE TWO APPLES PLUS TOW APPLES EQUALS FOUR APPLIES.
AND THE ANSWER OF ADDITION IS CALLED SUM.

SUBTRACTION:
SUBTRACTING SIMILER THINGS LIKE TWO APPLES MINUS TOW APPLES EQUALS ZERO APPLIES.
AND THE ANSWER OF SUBTRACTION IS CALLED DIFFERENCE.

MULTIPLICATION:
TO PREVENT UNNESSECERY REPATAION WE USE MULTILICATION.
EXAMPLE:INSTED OF 2+2+2+2+2=10 WE SAT 5x2=10.
THE ANSWER OF MULTIPLICATION IS CALLED PRODUCT.

DIVISION:
SEPERAT ONT THING INTO MANY PARTS.
EXAMPLE:8 DIVIDED BY 2 EQUALS 4 WHICH MEANS 8 IS DIVIDED INTO TWO GROUPS ADN EACH GROUP CONTAINS 4 NUMBERS.
THE ANSWER OF DIVISION IS CALLED QUATIENT.

NOTES:
1-ADDITION AND MUTIPLICATION HAVE COMMUTATIVE PROPERTY WHICH MEANS WE CAN SWITCH THE NUMBERS ADN GET SAME ANSWER.
BUT SUBTRACTION ADN DIVISION DO NOT.
2-UNDERSTANDIN THE ANSWERS OF OPERAION IS IMPORTANT BECAUSE IN SAME EXAM THE QUESTION IS LIKE THIS,
WHAT IS THE PRODUCT OF 4 AND 5? SO WHEN YOU DON`T KNOW THE MEANING OF PRODUCT HOW YOU ARE SUPPOSED TO SOLVE IT.
