# COMPUTER_SCIENCE_MATHEMATICS

## Goal

I created this repository to keep myself committed to preparing for the **Open Doors Scholarship**, which is my dream and long-term goal.

Mathematics is one of the most important subjects in Computer Science. I once heard, *"The more mathematics you learn, the better you become at solving problems."* I believe this is true, and I want to strengthen my mathematical foundation to become a better programmer and computer scientist.


## Concepts

My learning journey is divided into three phases.

### Phase 1: Mathematical Foundations

* Arithmetic
* Fractions
* Decimals
* Percentages
* Ratios
* Exponents
* Roots
* Absolute Value
* Polynomials
* Factorials
* Basic Geometry

### Phase 2: Algebra and Calculus

* Equations
* Inequalities
* Logarithms
* Functions
* Trigonometry
* Limits
* Derivatives
* Integrals

### Phase 3: Linear Algebra and Mathematics for Computer Science

#### Linear Algebra

* Vectors
* Matrices
* Matrix Operations
* Determinants

#### Discrete Mathematics

* Logic
* Sets
* Combinatorics
* Probability
* Graph Theory
* Breadth-First Search (BFS)
* Depth-First Search (DFS)

## Learning Method

For every topic, I will:

1. Study the lesson by watching high-quality educational videos.
2. Take notes on important concepts, formulas, and ideas.
3. Solve at least **25 practice exercises**.
4. Record the questions that I find difficult or cannot solve on my first attempt.
5. Document what I learned at the end of each day.
