SECOND LESSON IS FRACTION:
DIFENATION:
A FRACTION IS A WAY OF EXPRESSING PART OF WHOLE.
A/B=C A IS NUMERATOR,B IS DENOMERATO / DIVISION SIGN.

TYPES OF FRACTION:
1-PROPER:
A/B A MUST BE SMALLER THAN B.
2-IMPROPER:
A/B A MUST BE EQUALL OR BIGGER THAN B.
3-MIXED:
C+A/B C IS A WHOLE AND A/B IS PROPER FRACTION.

CONVERT IMPROPER INTO MIXED FRACTION:
8/3 IS A IMPROPER FRACTION S IF 8 IS DIVIDED BY 3 THE RESULT IS THIS 2+2/3 AND IT IS A MIXED FRACTION.

CONVERS MIXED FRACTION INTO IMPROPER:
C+A/B WE KNOW C IS QUOTIENT A RIMAINDER B DENOMINTOR THE FORMULA TO CONVERT IS C TIMES B PLUS A OVER B(CxB+A/B).

A/B+C/B=A+C/B

2-MULTIPLYING FRACTIONS:

COMPARING FRACTIONS:
1-IF THE NUMERATORS AND DENUNERATORS ARE EQUALS SO FRACTIONS ARE EQUAL.
2-IF DENUMERATORS ARE EQUAL FRACTION WHICH HAS BIGGER NUMERATORE IS BIGGER.
3-IF NUMERATORS ARE EQUAL FRACTION WHICH HAS SMALLER DENUMERATORE IS BIGGER.
4-IF BOTH NUMERATORS AND DENUMERATORS ARE DIFFERENT FISR FIND THE LOWEST COMMON DENUMERATOR ADN THEN COMPARE THEM.

TIPS:
1=A IMPROPER FRACTION IS BIGGER THAN ALL PROPER FRACTIONS.
2-IN ADDING AND SUBTRACTING FRACTIONS IF THE NUMERATORS AND DENUMERATORS ARE DIFFERENT FISR FIND THE
LOWEST COMMON DENUMERATOR AND THEN SOLVE IT
