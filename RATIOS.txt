MY FIFTH LESSON IS RATIONS:
DEFINITION:
RAIONS ARE EXPRESSIONS COMPARING ONE THING TO ANOTHER THING:
EX:IF WE HAVE 10 HORSES AND 5 DOS WE CAN SAY INSTED OF EVERY 5DOS WE HAVE 10 HORSES.
AND WE SHOE THE RAION LIKE THIS 10:5 10/5 OR 10 TO 5. AND WE REDUCE 10/5 WE CAM SAY INSTEAD OF EVERY DOG WE HAVE 2 HORSES.
TIP:
IF WE HAVE THE NUMBERS FINDING RATIOS IS EASY BUT WHEN WE HAVE RATION AND WANT TO FIND THE PRIME VALUE OF EACH UNIT 
IT WILL NOT EASY WE HAVE TO KNOW THE FORMULA.
EX: THE RATIO OF BOYS AND GIRLS IN A CLASS IS 2:3 AND THE NUMBER OF STUDENTS IS 40.
SO HOW WE CAN KNOW HOW MANY GIRSL OR BOYS WE HAVE?
LET`S FIND THE NUMBERS OF GIRSL WE GOT TO ADD THE BOYS` UNIT WHICH IS 2 AND THE GIRLS` UNIT WHICH IS 3 AND THEN WE MUST
DIVIDE THE TOTAL NUMBER(40) DIVIDED BY SUM OF BOYS AND GIRLS(5) NOW WE KNOW THAT WE HAVE 8 GROUPS INCLUDING 2 BOYS AND
3 GIRLS ADN FINALLY BY MULTIPLYING THE NUMBERS OF GROUP BY EACH UNIT WE KNOW THE HOW MANY GIRLS ADN BOYS WE HAVE,
THE NUMBER OF BOYS IS (8 TIMES 2) 16.
THE NUMBER OF GIRLS IS (8 TIMES 3) 24.


RROPORTION:
IT REFERS TO TWO EQUIVALENT RATIOS WITH SAME UNITS NOT SAME VALUES.
EX:1/2=5/10
WE USE IT TO FIND SOMTHING THAT IS NUKNOWN.
EX:A STUDENT READS A BOOK IN 2 DAYS (1/2) HOW MANY DAYS DOES HE TAKE TO READ 23 BOOKS.
THE QUESTION IS LIKE THIS 1/2=23/X (X MEANS UNKNOWN) SO WE USE CROSS_MULTIPLAYING TO FIND THE ANSWER.
1TIMES X=2 TIMES 23 FINAL ANSWER IS X=46.

NOTES:
1-CROSS_MULTIPLYIN FORMULA IS LIKE THIS WE MULTIPLY NUMERATOR OF FIRST FRACTION WITH DENUMERATO OF SECOND FRACTION ADN 
VISE VERSA.AND WE PUT X(UNKNOWN) AT ONE SIDE OF EQUIVALENT SIGN ADN KNOWN VALUE OTHER SIDE AND DIVIDED BOTH SIDES BY
NUMBER BEING MUTIPLYING WITH X.
